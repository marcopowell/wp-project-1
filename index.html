MTB: San Francisco Bay Area

A guide for Bay Area locals and visitors of all riding levels to discover some of the best mountain biking trails in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Explore what the Bay Area has to offer!

The Bay Area has some of the most popular mountain biking trails in California. The Bay Area is vast area with a large variety of terrain and land ranging from sandy shores to Redwood forests to dry, rocky and dusty hills or mountains and everything in between, which is what makes it an awesome place to ride. You will find all you need in the Bay Area -but if what you are looking for is not here- you are only a short drive away from flowy and fast trails in Santa Cruz, CA and a short road trip away from the NorthStar Bike Park in Lake, Tahoe.

This guide is made for new and expericned riders looking to experience new trails around the Bay Area. The trails on this website have been specifically picked as they are some of the most popular ones among locals.

In addition to being able to virtually explore the trails you can also join me along with many other riders of all skill levels on a weekly mountain bike ride to a new location. Sign up for an upcoming ride here.

MISSION STATEMENT

As we get through this pandemic and we find ways to spend our time outdoors whenever possible, my goal is to share my knowledge with people that might be thinking about getting into mountain biking to persuade them to make that final step to begin riding the trails. At the same time, to show new riders and experienced riders, local or from afar, new and exciting places they could explore on two wheels.
