Best Trails in the SF Bay Area

Joaquin Miller Park - Oakland, CA

Joaquin Miller Park, or JMP as it is also known, is located in the Oakland hills and is easily accesible to anyone in the East Bay. The park has a variety of mountain biking trails that are also shared with hikers. Most of the trails in this park are rated as easy but there are a few intermediate trails. This place is good for beginners looking to improve their skill levels.

China Camp Park - San Rafael, CA

China Camp State Park, just like most other state and regional parks, has trails for everyone including mountain bikers. Most of the trails at China Camp are for intermediate riders given its rocky and narrow trails with dangerous steep ravines. However, there are also some easy trails. This is a good place for an intermediate rider to improve. There is a park entry fee, so make sure you pay it at the trailhead or you could be cited.
